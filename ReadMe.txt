January 14, 2015

This zip archive contains two Word documents for each of 44 information and communication technology (ICT) products and services:  a Government Product/Service Accessibility Template (GPAT) and procurement solicitation language that references the GPAT.  These documents are prepared by the U.S. General Services Administration as technical assistance for implementing Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.  The documents were obtained from BuyAccessible.gov using the ICT terms and download URLs listed below:
